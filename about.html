Our Story

We got tired of watching software projects fail

Not because of bad developers. Because of bad approaches.

After 25+ years of building software across industries, we saw the same patterns repeat. Projects start fast, then slow to a crawl. Complexity compounds. Budgets explode. Teams burn out. Everyone blames the technology, but the real problem is structural.

Sliceworkz was born from a simple conviction: software development doesn't have to be this way. We took everything we learned -- domain-driven design, event modeling, vertical slice architecture, event sourcing -- and rebuilt our entire practice around them. Not as add-ons. As the foundation.

Then AI changed the game. Instead of bolting AI onto old processes, we designed Sliceworkz from scratch to be AI-native. Every methodology we use, every project structure we build, is optimized for AI-assisted development. The result: software that stays maintainable, teams that stay productive, and costs that grow linearly -- not exponentially.

We're not a large consultancy. We're a focused team that knows exactly what works and isn't afraid to say what doesn't. No fluff. No overengineering. Just battle-tested methods, applied with precision.

The Team

We have built and shipped large-scale enterprise applications and complex integration projects across industries, working with Java, Spring Boot, modern frontend technologies, DevOps, Cloud, and AI.
