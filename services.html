Software Delivery Services

From modernization to greenfield. We deliver software that works for your business.

What we do

Four core services covering the full lifecycle of enterprise software, from rescuing legacy systems to building the next generation.

1

Legacy Modernization

Breathe new life into legacy systems without starting from scratch. We incrementally modernize monoliths and outdated architectures using vertical slice architecture, event sourcing, and domain-driven design, preserving business logic while eliminating technical debt and exponential maintenance costs.

2

Greenfield Development

Greenfield projects built right from day one. We design and deliver custom enterprise applications grounded in your business domain, using AI-first development and event modeling to ensure fast time-to-market, maintainability, and a codebase that scales at linear cost as your business grows.

3

Digitize & Automate

Transform manual and paper-based business processes into streamlined digital workflows. We help organizations identify high-impact digitalization opportunities and deliver tailored solutions that automate operations, improve data visibility, and unlock new business capabilities across the enterprise.

4

Evolutive Maintenance

Keep your applications running smoothly and evolving with your business. We provide ongoing maintenance, performance monitoring, bug resolution, and incremental feature delivery, ensuring your software stays reliable, secure, and aligned with changing requirements without accumulating accidental complexity.
